# Voice Commands Guide

## How to Use Voice Commands

1. **Tap the microphone button** (bottom-right corner) to start listening
2. **Speak clearly** after the button turns red
3. The app will automatically process your command
4. The microphone will stop listening after you finish speaking

## Available Commands

### Radio Controls
- **"Play radio"** - Start playing the first radio station
- **"Play Hindi"** - Play Hindi radio station
- **"Play Tamil"** - Play Tamil radio station
- **"Play Telugu"** - Play Telugu radio station
- **"Play Malayalam"** - Play Malayalam radio station
- **"Play Kannada"** - Play Kannada radio station
- **"Play Punjabi"** - Play Punjabi radio station
- **"Play Bengali"** - Play Bengali radio station
- **"Play Bollywood"** - Play Bollywood music
- **"Play Kollywood"** - Play Tamil cinema music
- **"Play Tollywood"** - Play Telugu cinema music

### Playback Controls
- **"Stop"** - Stop playback
- **"Pause"** - Pause current playback
- **"Resume"** or **"Continue"** - Resume playback
- **"Next"** - Skip to next station/track
- **"Previous"** or **"Back"** - Go to previous station/track

### Other Commands
- **"Play music"** - Start playing music
- **"Play podcast"** - Start playing podcasts
- **"Suggest"** or **"Recommend"** - Get AI-powered station recommendations

## Tips for Best Results

1. Speak clearly and at a normal pace
2. Reduce background noise when using voice commands
3. Make sure microphone permissions are granted
4. Wait for the red indicator before speaking
5. Use simple, direct commands

## Troubleshooting

- **Voice not recognized?** Check microphone permissions in Settings
- **Commands not working?** Try speaking more clearly or closer to the device
- **Button not responding?** Restart the app and try again
